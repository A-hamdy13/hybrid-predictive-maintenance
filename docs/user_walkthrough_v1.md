# Hybrid Predictive Maintenance - Quick Walkthrough (v1)

## Driver side
1. Log in as driver and pick your assigned vehicle.
2. Open Maintenance Cards to enter/update routine or failure events.
3. Fill operational inputs (and telemetry if ML is enabled).
4. Choose schedule mode (normal or severe), then click Get recommendation.
5. Review priority, explanation, and nearby mechanics if urgent.
6. Use history/simplified views to track past maintenance states.

## Admin side
1. Log in as admin to manage drivers, vehicles, and affiliates.
2. Add/edit vehicle rules from extracted manuals or manual entry.
3. Verify service intervals and update rules when manuals change.
4. Assign vehicles to drivers and maintain service center listings.

## Tips
- Rule-based outputs are deterministic; ML is optional risk support.
- Keep maintenance cards updated for better recommendations.
- Use CSV/PDF exports for reporting and audits.
